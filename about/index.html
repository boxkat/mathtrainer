endless

In endless mode, your goal is to get as many questions right as you can.

The game will only end when you click Stop.

You will only be shown your results at the end of the game.

20 questions

In 20 questions mode, you will be given 20 questions.

Your goal is to get as many of these right as possible.

Again, you will only be shown your results at the end of the game.
